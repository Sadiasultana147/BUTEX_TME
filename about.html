Department of Textile Materials Engineering

Welcome to the Department of Textile Materials Engineering (TME), Bangladesh University of Textiles (BUTEX). TME is an emerging and highly demanding field of study. It is an interdisciplinary field comprising engineering, chemistry and environment. The faculty is committed to teaching, research and public service. A number of faculty members hold doctoral degrees in a variety of related fields. The faculty is enriched each year by new regular faculty appointees as well as return of faculty after completing higher education and research abroad. This scholarly talents adds freshness and vitality to the department of TME. The TME program will be recognized as the premier international program for preparing young men and women engineers for the textile industry and beyond. This recognition will be based upon the excellence of entering students, faculty, resources, educational experiences, student and faculty diversity, and impact on the industry. We earnestly emphasize on a healthy and intimate teacher- student relationship so that the true potential of each student is comprehended. The relationship of the department with the local industries is strengthened through various programs such as seminars, workshop, conferences and different events. The department of TME aims not only to produce efficient engineers, but also well-educated reliable leaders who can contribute to the development of the country through enriching our industries.

The Department of Textile Materials Engineering (DoTME) mainly focuses on introducing advanced functional textile materials and synthesizing new hybrid materials for advancements in textile education. Textile Materials Engineering combines textile engineering, chemistry, physics, and material science to investigate the characteristics and behavior of materials for novel improved functional textile materials. The development of wearable textile technologies contributes to enhanced communication, information access, and connectivity by leading advanced materials. In addition, the department also focuses on synthesizing biomaterials not only on mitigating climate change, using joint replacements, and cardiovascular stents, and improving the fuel efficiency of automotive industries. The Department of Textile Materials Engineering (DoTME) curriculum focuses on the synthesis of advanced materials, textile material chemistry, fiber-forming polymers, structural properties, and functional behavior. This department's main strengths are the most advanced laboratory machinery, skilled staff, and qualified faculty members. This department invites outstanding and exceptionally driven students to consider our variety of interesting and intriguing research opportunities for the betterment of our nation and society. The department reflects BUTEX's commitment to research and industry collaboration. Academic learning combines strong theoretical foundations with intensive practical programs such as laboratory experiments, internships, in-house projects, and joint projects with industry partners. The curriculum covers fibre-forming polymer engineering, fibre characterization, and a wide spectrum of textile material science topics designed to produce industry-ready graduates.

  • Practical Labs — advanced lab work and instrumentation training.
  • Industry Internships — real projects with dyeing & finishing firms.
  • Research & Collaboration — active research and technology transfer.
