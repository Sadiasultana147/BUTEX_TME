🔬 Advanced Research Trinocular Brightfield Microscope

High-resolution imaging for material analysis & research

Overview

This advanced research-grade microscope is designed for textile materials engineering and scientific research. It integrates high-resolution optics with digital imaging, allowing researchers to observe microscopic structures and capture detailed images for analysis.

The system is connected with specialized software for live imaging, measurement, and analysis, making it highly effective for fiber characterization, defect detection, and material property study.

Educational and laboratory microscope for routine applications. Dye-cast frame, with high stability and ergonomic, for transmitted light observation. The use of a modern optical microscope allows for much higher magnification, reduced chromatic aberration and exchangeable objective lenses to adjust the magnification. This also enables more advanced illumination setups such as phase contrast.
