State-of-the-Art Laboratories

The TME Department at BUTEX provides students with modern lab facilities, including fiber characterization, polymer processing, and textile testing laboratories. Every lab is equipped with advanced machinery and instruments to ensure practical learning.

Our labs are designed to simulate real industrial environments, giving students the skills required to excel in the textile industry.

Hands-On Practical Experience

Students get hands-on experience with yarn engineering, fabric testing, dyeing, and finishing processes under expert supervision. Practical exposure ensures theoretical concepts are thoroughly understood.

Collaboration with industries and research projects in our labs give students real-world insights and enhance problem-solving skills.

Textile Materials Engineering Department — Lab Facilities
