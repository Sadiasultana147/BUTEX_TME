The Department of Textile Materials Engineering (DoTME) is dedicated to advancing textile sciences through education, research, and industry collaboration. We foster innovation, sustainability, and excellence, producing graduates ready to impact the textile industry globally.

Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in textile materials engineering, advancing knowledge through innovation, research, and education.

  • Academic Excellence: Deliver world-class education to produce skilled, innovative textile engineers.
  • Research Leadership: Pioneer groundbreaking research in textile material chemistry and advanced functional textiles.
  • Industry Impact: Drive technology transfer and sustainable solutions for the textile sector.
“Empowering innovation and sustainable progress in textile materials science.”

Mission

To nurture innovation and sustainability through outcome-based education, high-impact research, and industry partnerships.

  • Modernizing of existing education system and curriculum through incorporating outcome-based education system and curriculum along with highly skilled academicians and staff.
  • Developing adequate infrastructure facilities to enable smooth running of academic program through ensuring quality.
  • Graduating talented, broadly educated engineers to explore, exploit, utilize and conserve available resources for sustainable development of Textile sector.
  • Conducting fundamental and applied quality research in the field of chemical processing of textiles like wet preparation, dyeing, printing, finishing and environmental issues related to textile wet treatments.
  • Developing breakthrough technologies through local and global collaboration with industries and/or other institution.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging creative ideas and technologies to advance textile science.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly materials and processes for a better future.

Collaboration

Building strong partnerships with industry, academia, and communities.

Excellence

Striving for highest standards in teaching, research, and service.

Combining tradition with innovation, the Department of Textile Materials Engineering empowers students and researchers with the knowledge, skills, and opportunities to lead the textile industry into a sustainable and technologically advanced future.
